The slanting buildings

Reminds me of the

Rocket Domed tower

One saw while coming from Hebbal to Marathahalli

Daily crossing Marathahalli bridge was a pain made by everyone

Now running across

Konrad Adenauer Brucke

Is my Trial By Fire
