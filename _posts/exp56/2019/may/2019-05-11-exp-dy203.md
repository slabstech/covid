Life is like

Running

Around a Circle

Holding on

Dearly to a Thread

At the Centre

Take your eyes(hands :P) off the thread

Your thrown away

Like a long tangent far far away

from where you wished

To Go

Dont Let Go

Dont fall off the Pedestal
